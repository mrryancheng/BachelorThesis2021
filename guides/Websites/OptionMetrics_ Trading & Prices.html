OptionMetrics: Trading & Prices

OptionMetrics: Trading & Prices

Detail

According to OptionMetrics, all greeks are calculated using the spot price, which is the close price you find in the Security Prices table. And you can't find forward price anywhere using OptionMetrics data. 

Equity options actually trade in the synchronized fashion as the stocks exchanges and the hour is 8:30 am to 3:00 pm central time. See CBOE site for more info. 

Having said that, please be aware that there are non-synchronization issue with the way OptionMetrics collects option prices. Here's how OptionMetrics explains their procedure: 

" Option prices used in implied volatility calculations up to March 4, 2008 are end of day prices (i.e. 4:15 PM EST). Starting from March 5, 2008 we have been capturing best bid and best offer as close to 4 oclock as possible in an attempt to better synchronize the option price with the underlying close. Currently the option prices are captured at 15:59 EST. The underlying price used is the official (composite) close which can be found in Security_Price file. "
